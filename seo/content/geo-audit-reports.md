## Section: What is a GEO Audit? (And Why Every Business Needs One in 2026)
GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimisation — the practice of making your business visible in AI-generated search results, not just traditional search engine rankings. A GEO audit is a systematic review of how your business currently appears (or doesn't appear) when people ask AI tools questions relevant to your services and location. It covers what information the AI platforms have about your business, whether that information is accurate, and what signals would increase the chances of you being recommended. Think of it as a health check for your presence in the new search landscape. Traditional SEO is still important — but GEO is the layer on top that growing numbers of customers are now using to make decisions.

## FAQ

**What is GEO?**
GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimisation. It refers to the practice of making your business visible in AI-generated search results — the answers that tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity generate when someone asks a question relevant to your services.

**Is GEO the same as SEO?**
They're related but different. Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in search engine results pages (like Google's blue links). GEO focuses on being cited and recommended by AI-generated answers. Both matter in 2026, but most businesses have only thought about one of them.

**Which AI platforms do you audit?**
We audit ChatGPT (OpenAI), Google Gemini, Perplexity AI, and Google AI Overviews — the four platforms with the highest adoption in Australia and the greatest impact on local business discovery.

**How long does it take to improve AI search visibility?**
Some fixes — like updating your website's structured data and correcting inconsistent business information — can have an effect within weeks. Content-based improvements typically take one to three months to be picked up and reflected in AI responses. The audit report prioritises actions by speed of impact.

**Does my business need to be on the Sunshine Coast?**
Our GEO audits are available to businesses across Australia, though our primary focus is Sunshine Coast and Queensland businesses.

**What happens after the audit?**
The report gives you a clear action plan you can implement yourself, or we can implement the recommendations for you as part of an ongoing GEO optimisation engagement. Many clients also combine the GEO audit with our AI lead generation or AI website building service to maximise the impact.
