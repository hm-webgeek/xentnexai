## Overview

This document covers keyword research and naming recommendations for two service pages where the client has requested a rename:

1. `/ai-voice-agents` ‚Äî currently "AI Voice Agents"
2. `/ai-website-building` ‚Äî currently "AI Website Building"

## Page 1 ‚Äî AI Voice Agents (`/ai-voice-agents`)

### Terms Researched

| Term | AU Market Signal | Verdict |
|---|---|---|
| AI Voice Agents | Low consumer awareness; technical/developer framing | Weak for local SME audience |
| AI Receptionist | High and growing; multiple AU-dedicated providers; clear consumer intent | Strong |
| Conversational AI | Enterprise/developer framing; used by vendors, not buyers | Weak for local SME audience |
| AI Phone Answering | Moderate; used by national comparison content (TransferToAI, GetApp AU) | Decent secondary term |
| Virtual Receptionist | Established but not AI-specific; dominated by OfficeHQ, Ruby, SEQVA | Useful secondary only |

### Competitive Landscape (AU)

"AI receptionist Australia" is a well-established and growing search cluster. The market has multiple national product players ‚Äî Johnni.ai, Sophiie AI, TransferToAI, Hooroo.ai, AiDial, OfficeHQ ‚Äî all of whom have staked out "AI receptionist" as their category name. This is a signal that the term has already won the category definition battle in the AU market.

Key finding for local positioning: **OfficeHQ is Sunshine Coast-based** (Buderim, QLD) and dominates "answering service Sunshine Coast" with significant domain authority. However, they are a national SaaS product, not a local agency offering a managed/customised AI solution. DigitalOn (Sunshine Coast) has an AI receptionist product page but their positioning is broad digital agency, not AI-specialist.

**"AI receptionist Sunshine Coast"** is searched locally and has zero dedicated local-first competition. XentnexAI can own this phrase.

"Conversational AI" surfaces enterprise vendors (AWS, IBM, Salesforce), B2B SaaS tools, and top-100 company lists ‚Äî entirely the wrong intent for a local service page. It would not attract trades or clinic owners searching for a call-answering solution.

"AI phone answering" appears as a secondary/supporting phrase in national comparison content. It is valid as a secondary keyword but does not carry the same recognisability or intent clarity as "AI receptionist."

### Recommendation

**Recommended page name / H1 term: AI Receptionist**

This is the term that:
- Local business owners (tradies, clinic managers) already use when searching
- All major AU providers have coalesced around
- Has clear transactional intent ("I need something to answer my phones")
- Is plain-language ‚Äî suits the non-technical audience
- Allows natural local modifier: "AI Receptionist Sunshine Coast"

#### Primary Keyword
`AI receptionist Sunshine Coast`

#### Secondary Keywords
1. `AI phone answering service Sunshine Coast`
2. `24/7 AI receptionist trades Queensland`
3. `AI receptionist for small business Australia`
4. `never miss a call AI Queensland`
5. `AI receptionist dental clinic tradies Sunshine Coast`

#### Search Intent
**Commercial / transactional.** High urgency ‚Äî users are problem-aware ("I miss calls on the tools," "my clinic can't afford a full-time receptionist"). They are not researching the technology; they are shopping for a solution. Page must address pain, demonstrate capability, and convert.

#### Competition Level (AU)
- National: **Medium‚ÄìHigh** (Sophiie, Johnni.ai, TransferToAI, Hooroo.ai, OfficeHQ all active)
- Local "Sunshine Coast" modifier: **Low** ‚Äî no dedicated local-agency competitor owns this phrase
- DigitalOn is the closest Sunshine Coast competitor with an AI receptionist page; XentnexAI's advantage is deeper local positioning and AI-specialist branding

#### Reasoning

"AI receptionist" has won the category naming war in Australia. Every credible AU provider ‚Äî including those built specifically for tradies ‚Äî uses this term. Consumers searching for a phone-answering AI in Australia type "AI receptionist," not "AI voice agent" (too technical), "conversational AI" (too enterprise), or "AI phone answering" (less established). Adopting "AI Receptionist" as the page name aligns XentnexAI with the language the audience already uses, and the "Sunshine Coast" local modifier makes the primary keyword highly winnable.

#### Meta Title
`AI Receptionist Sunshine Coast | Never Miss a Call`
*(51 characters)*

#### Meta Description
`24/7 AI receptionist for Sunshine Coast trades, clinics & local businesses. Book appointments, qualify leads, answer every call. Get a free demo today.`
*(152 characters ‚Äî en-AU, CTA included)*

### Slug Risk Flag

**The existing slug `/ai-voice-agents` will not match the new name "AI Receptionist."**

Options:
1. **Rename the slug** to `/ai-receptionist` ‚Äî cleanest SEO alignment; use a 301 redirect from `/ai-voice-agents` to `/ai-receptionist`
2. **Keep the old slug** ‚Äî avoids redirect overhead but creates a mismatch between URL and page name, which is a mild negative signal

**Recommendation: rename the slug to `/ai-receptionist` and implement a 301 redirect.** The page has no inbound links or ranking history worth preserving at `/ai-voice-agents` yet, so a clean rename now has zero downside and long-term keyword alignment benefit.

## Page 2 ‚Äî AI Website Building (`/ai-website-building`)

### Terms Researched

| Term | AU Market Signal | Verdict |
|---|---|---|
| Website Generation | Near-zero consumer searches; no established category use | Avoid |
| AI Website Building | Emerging; used as a descriptor; low competition but also low search volume | Weak primary |
| AI Website Design | Moderate and growing; maps to clear buyer intent; used by AU competitors | Strong |
| AI Web Design | Less common in AU; "web design" without "website" is slightly less natural for local SME searches | Decent secondary |
| AI-Powered Website Design | Descriptive but too long for primary keyword targeting | Use as body copy, not H1 |

### Competitive Landscape (Sunshine Coast)

"Website design Sunshine Coast" is a highly contested local phrase: DIGITALON, White Peak Digital, Social Tap, Sunshine Coast Web Design (est. 1998), Success Marketing, Website Builder Australia, and others all hold positions. This generic phrase is very hard to win for a new entrant.

"AI website design Sunshine Coast" is a notably different sub-niche. As of April 2026:
- DIGITALON is the only Sunshine Coast competitor with explicit AI + website design positioning
- All others are traditional web agencies that mention AI as a feature, not a specialisation
- The "AI website design" modifier cuts competition significantly while maintaining commercial intent

"Website Generation" returned no relevant AU consumer results. The term appears in the context of AI developer tooling (code generation, HTML generation) ‚Äî not in the consumer context of "I need a website for my business." Adopting this term would mean educating the market from zero, with no search traffic to intercept. **This term should not be used.**

### Recommendation

**Recommended page name / H1 term: AI Website Design**

This term:
- Maps directly to how local SMEs search when they want a website built
- Pairs naturally with the "Sunshine Coast" local modifier
- Differentiates from generic "website design" by leading with the AI angle
- Is used by at least one Sunshine Coast competitor (DIGITALON), confirming there is a real local audience for this phrase
- Has medium competition nationally but low competition at the "AI website design Sunshine Coast" local level

#### Primary Keyword
`AI website design Sunshine Coast`

#### Secondary Keywords
1. `AI web design Sunshine Coast`
2. `AI-powered website Sunshine Coast small business`
3. `affordable AI website design Queensland`
4. `website design for trades health Sunshine Coast`
5. `fast website build AI Queensland`

#### Search Intent
**Commercial / transactional.** Users either need a first website or want to upgrade an existing one. Price and speed are key qualifiers. The "AI" modifier adds a differentiation signal ‚Äî buyers are open to a modern, faster approach vs a traditional agency. SME owners on the Sunshine Coast are price-conscious; "AI-powered" implies efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

#### Competition Level (AU)
- Generic "website design Sunshine Coast": **High** (10+ established local competitors)
- "AI website design Sunshine Coast": **Low‚ÄìMedium** ‚Äî DIGITALON is the primary local competitor with this specific positioning; all others use AI as a feature mention, not a brand anchor
- National "AI website design Australia": **Medium** ‚Äî growing competition from SaaS-style AI builders (Wix AI, Squarespace AI, ChilledSites, Durable) but these are DIY tools, not agency services ‚Äî different buyer intent

#### Reasoning

"Website Generation" as a term has essentially no consumer search activity in Australia. No business owner types "website generation" when looking for a web agency ‚Äî they type "website design" or "web design." Adopting a zero-volume phrase would mean relying entirely on paid or social traffic, with no organic upside.

"AI Website Design" beats "AI Website Building" because:
- "Design" is the dominant word in how buyers describe this purchase decision
- "Building" is how agencies describe their process, not how buyers describe their need
- "AI website design" has more national search activity than "AI website building" in the AU market
- Aligns with DIGITALON's existing positioning ‚Äî confirming the phrase has local market relevance

The page should continue to include "building" language in body copy to capture both variants, but the primary keyword and H1 anchor should be "AI Website Design."

#### Meta Title
`AI Website Design Sunshine Coast | XentnexAI`
*(45 characters)*

#### Meta Description
`AI-powered websites for Sunshine Coast businesses. Fast builds, smart design, built to convert. Get a free quote from your local AI agency today.`
*(145 characters ‚Äî en-AU, CTA included)*

### Slug Risk Flag

**The existing slug `/ai-website-building` is a partial mismatch for the recommended name "AI Website Design."**

The word "building" in the slug vs "design" in the H1 and primary keyword is a mild but real SEO misalignment. Options:

1. **Rename the slug** to `/ai-website-design` ‚Äî best keyword alignment; 301 redirect from `/ai-website-building`
2. **Keep the old slug** ‚Äî preserves any existing link equity (minimal at this stage); the mismatch is small enough that it won't harm rankings significantly

**Recommendation: rename the slug to `/ai-website-design` and 301 redirect from `/ai-website-building`.** The page is relatively new with no significant inbound link history, so now is the cheapest time to make this change.
