## Research Notes

### Competitive Landscape (Sunshine Coast AI / Digital)
Local competitors identified: Tech Horizon Labs (Sunshine Coast AI consulting), Practical AI Integration (Sunshine Coast automation), Connectivity Marketing (Sunshine Coast digital marketing + AI), DIGITALON (Sunshine Coast web design + AI). Broader QLD competitors: April Ford (Brisbane + SC), ConvertMate, Digital Nomads HQ.

**Key insight:** No competitor in the local market is explicitly targeting all three signals — AI automation + local SEO + GEO/AI search visibility — under a single Sunshine Coast brand. XentnexAI has a differentiation window, particularly for GEO Audit Reports (zero dedicated local competition found) and the combination positioning of "AI agency Sunshine Coast."

### GEO / AI Search Landscape (2026)
GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) is a growing but still low-awareness search term in Australia. Most searches are phrased as "ChatGPT SEO," "AI search visibility," or "how to appear in ChatGPT." Volume is low nationally but rising fast; competition is extremely low at the local level. XentnexAI can own this space early.

### AI Voice Agents
High search activity in Australia for "AI receptionist" and "never miss a call" — multiple national competitors (OfficeHQ, Sophiie, Johnni.ai, AppyTradies). Local (Sunshine Coast-specific) positioning is wide open. Targeting trades + medical is the highest-intent angle.

### AI Lead Generation
Moderate competition at national level (Marketing Eye, April Ford). Low competition for local "Sunshine Coast" modifier. Strong intent signals from phrases like "stop missing enquiries," "automated lead capture," "AI chatbot for small business."

### AI Website Building
Very competitive nationally for "website design Sunshine Coast" but pure "AI website building" as a positioning angle is still emerging. Differentiation via speed, AI-native build process, and SME-friendly framing.

## Page 4 — GEO Audit Reports (/geo-audit-reports)

### Primary Keyword
**GEO audit Sunshine Coast** *(primary local term)*

**Note on keyword strategy:** "GEO audit" as a standalone phrase has very low search volume globally and essentially zero volume at local level — this is a first-mover opportunity, not a high-volume play. The page must also rank for the **informational** terms people ARE searching: "how to appear in ChatGPT," "AI search visibility audit," "get found in AI search Australia." The page should educate AND convert.

### Secondary Keywords
- AI search visibility audit Australia
- appear in ChatGPT for my business
- generative engine optimisation Australia
- how to rank in AI search results
- ChatGPT SEO for small business
- Perplexity Gemini visibility audit
- GEO vs SEO Australia 2026

### Search Intent
**Informational → Commercial** — most users don't know the term "GEO audit" yet. They're searching "why doesn't my business show up in ChatGPT" or "AI search optimisation." The page must bridge awareness → education → purchase intent.

### Competition Level
**Very Low (local), Low (national).** This is a genuinely new search space. Valantic, Authority AI, and a handful of international agencies offer GEO audits, but almost no Australian agency (and certainly no Sunshine Coast agency) has a dedicated service page and keyword strategy around this. XentnexAI can establish authority now while competition is minimal.

### Recommended H1
**Does Your Business Show Up When Customers Ask ChatGPT? Find Out With a GEO Audit.**

*(Reframes the problem in plain language the audience understands; the word "ChatGPT" is the hook; "GEO Audit" is introduced naturally.)*
