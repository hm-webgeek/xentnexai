XENTNEXAI BRANDING BRIEF
========================

Business
--------
XentnexAI is an AI services agency focused on the Sunshine Coast, Queensland, Australia.
The audience is local Sunshine Coast business owners (trades, health, real estate, hospitality,
professional services) who are not necessarily technical.

Three Services
--------------
1. AI Lead Generation System — automated lead capture, qualification, and nurture sequences
2. AI Voice Agents — 24/7 AI-powered call answering, lead qualification, and appointment booking
3. GEO Audit Reports — audits how a business appears in ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity,
   with a prioritised action plan to improve AI search visibility

Desired Feel
------------
Clean, modern, light and fresh. Approachable and trustworthy — not cold or corporate.
Should feel local and personal, not like a faceless tech giant.

Existing Colours (to evolve, not scrap)
----------------------------------------
- Teal accent:      #2DD4BF  (keep as signature colour)
- Gold/amber:       #E8A020
- Dark backgrounds: #0B1426, #0F1D35 (site is currently dark — explore light-mode-first)
- Body text:        #94A3B8

Direction Note
--------------
The existing site skews dark navy. The new brand guidelines should explore a lighter, fresher
direction — light-mode-first — while keeping teal as the hero colour. Consider what a confident,
sun-drenched, coastal-tech brand looks like at full brightness.

Image Generation
----------------
For any raster image generation (logos, mockups, visual concepts), use ComfyUI with FLUX.1-schnell
running locally. The model is already installed:

- UNet:  ~/ComfyUI/models/unet/flux1-schnell-Q8_0.gguf
- CLIP:  ~/ComfyUI/models/clip/clip_l.safetensors
         ~/ComfyUI/models/clip/t5xxl_fp8.safetensors
- VAE:   ~/ComfyUI/models/vae/flux_ae.safetensors

Start ComfyUI if not running: cd ~/ComfyUI && python main.py
API endpoint: http://127.0.0.1:8188

Use the ComfyUI API to submit workflows for image generation. Do NOT use external image
generation services.

Deliverables
------------
Output all files to: /workspace/extra/miniclaw/xentnexai/brand/

- brand-guidelines.md  — brand name rationale, voice/tone, positioning statement
- colours.md           — full palette (primary, secondary, accent) with hex, RGB, HSL and usage rules
- typography.md        — font pairings (Google Fonts preferred), scale, usage rules
- logo.svg             — a clean, usable SVG logo (wordmark, icon, or combination mark)
- logo-concepts.md     — rationale for the chosen direction, alternative concepts considered
